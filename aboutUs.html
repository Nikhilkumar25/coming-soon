ABOUT US

Let's know who we are

KALAYAAM is the platform for Live Artistic Courses. Unlike non-interactive and recorded video lectures, we are here to deliver a premium and practical Learning Experience through small groups of 10-15 people in each course.

Learners can learn directly from the best artists pursuing their career in a field and are here to share their learnings to boost the knowledge and the career of learners.

✔Credible Content - Course Content is designed and delivered live by verified industry experts with years of experience.

✔Micro-Communities - Our group-based approach creates the conditions for communities to emerge organically. We provided course-specific and course agnostic activities to help learners bond and form long-lasting relationships. eg before even the course begins, we conduct ice-breaker activities and introductions.

✔Personal feedback - Small groups make it much easier for an expert to provide feedback to all learners.

✔Agile curriculum - The world is changing fast, and so we make sure our content & curriculum is always updated with the latest knowledge.

✔Live Q&A with instructors - We have a special focus on this and make sure the learners leave with all their questions answered.

The platform will also be an opportunity for the artists to connect with their juniors, teach them what they have learned and helped them come forward.

We provide all the necessary tools and a trusted platform to build a high-quality live small group based course and be able to reach their audience without any geographical constraints.

KALAYAAM’s mission is to provide anyone interested in learning Creative Skills with like-minded people and community, high-quality content and access to expert guidance.

We would love to know your thoughts about us. If you have some advice or just want to say Hi, contact us at kalayaam.social@google.com, kalayaam@kalayaam.com
